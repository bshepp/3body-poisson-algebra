## Field-by-field notes

- **Offset 0,1**: index starts at 0 (matching `L_0`); the second offset
  value `1` is the index of the first term whose absolute value
  exceeds 1, i.e. `a(0) = 3`. The OEIS server fills the second value
  automatically; we list it for completeness.
- **Keyword `more`**: deliberately included because `a(4)` is not yet
  exact. Once it is, we update the entry and remove `more`.
- **Keyword `nice`**: an editorial preference. The sequence is striking
  precisely because of its universality across potentials and spatial
  dimensions. Editors may choose to remove `nice` per OEIS convention
  (only well-established editors apply it).
- **Cross-references** to `AYYYYYY` and `AZZZZZZ` are placeholders for
  our other in-flight submissions; replace with real A-numbers as they
  are assigned.

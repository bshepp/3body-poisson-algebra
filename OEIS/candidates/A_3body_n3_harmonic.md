## Quick summary

- **Sequence:** `3, 6, 13, 15, 15`
- **Meaning:** dimension of the level-`n` truncation of the Lie algebra
  generated under the Poisson bracket by the three pairwise interaction
  Hamiltonians of a planar 3-body system with **harmonic** pairwise
  potential V(r) = rÂ².
- **Closure:** the algebra closes at dimension **15**; all subsequent
  levels are also 15. Verified at L=4 in Mathematica with 11,937
  candidate brackets in 31.55 seconds.
- **Contrast with the singular case:** unlike the 1/r-class sequence
  `[3, 6, 17, 116]` (which keeps growing rapidly), the harmonic
  potential gives a finite-dimensional Lie algebra. This finiteness
  reflects the integrable structure of the harmonic interaction.
- **OEIS novelty:** `seq:3,6,13,15` returns only an unrelated factorial
  sequence (A215972) which begins with these four numbers by coincidence
  but diverges at term 5 (15 vs 26). Our sequence is novel.
